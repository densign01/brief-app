Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable Brief in the Share menu?

On iPhone/iPad: Open Safari, tap the Share button, scroll the app row, tap "More", then toggle Brief on.

On Mac: In Safari, click Share, select "More...", then check Brief to enable it.

Or visit our setup guide for step-by-step instructions.

Brief doesn't appear in my Share menu

Make sure you've opened the Brief app at least once and entered your email. Then follow the setup steps above to enable the extension.

I'm not receiving emails

Check your spam/junk folder. Emails come from brief@send-brief.com. If you still don't see them, double-check the email address in Brief's settings.

How do AI summaries work?

When enabled, Brief uses Google Gemini to generate a quick summary of the article. The summary highlights key points so you can decide if it's worth reading later. You can toggle this off in the share sheet if you prefer.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your email is stored only on your device. We don't store your articles or create accounts. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What links work with Brief?

Brief works with any URL you can share: news articles, blog posts, tweets, LinkedIn posts, product pages, YouTube videos, and more.
